Project Apex : Financial Model & Summary Dashboard

Built a 207-month project finance model for a renewable energy project, covering a 3-month preliminary period, 24-month construction, and 15-year operational phase, including a 24-month moratorium and capitalized interest during construction.

Cases were modeled independently around inflation, price increment rate, and capacity utilization. The model includes monthly and annual financial statements, properly balanced projections, and a project analysis sheet for key metrics and sensitivity insights,also included is a summary dashboard enabling informed investment decisions.
